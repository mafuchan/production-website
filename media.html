Welcome to the World of Cardcaptor Sakura!

If you have made it this far, then you possibly are an otaku or just someone curious about the magical girl "shojo" genre.
The story revolves around Sakura Kinomoto and a magical book full of cards that she discovers in her father's library. Throughout the series she must recover the cards that have scattered, learn how to use them and eventually make them her own. Throughout the website, you can learn about the differences between the anime and manga and search for the various cards that she uses.
